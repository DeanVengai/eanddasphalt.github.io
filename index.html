ABOUT US

Tar surfacing is the area of expertise for the newly established construction company, E and D Asphalt Ltd. Our offerings include clearing the site, building the base, laying durable asphalt, marking the roads for parking spaces, potholes, accessible areas, and dumping zones, as well as setting up tennis courts. In order to withstand extreme weather, whether it be bitterly cold winters or warm, humid seaside regions, we deliver safe, smooth, and durable asphalt that lasts forever.
